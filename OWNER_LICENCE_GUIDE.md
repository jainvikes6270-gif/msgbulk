# LATHAEPS SMART – Owner Licence Guide

## Plans

- New installation: 7-day free trial
- Paid user: ₹700 for 365 days
- Selected user: Free Lifetime
- One activation works only on the Device ID for which it was generated.

## Give a customer yearly or free access

1. Paid customer opens **Settings → Plan & Licence → PAY ₹700 VIA UPI** and pays to `jainvikes6270@oksbi`.
2. Ask the customer to send the payment screenshot and displayed Device ID.
3. On the owner's app, open **Settings → Plan & Licence → OWNER TOOLS**.
4. Enter the private Owner PIN: `LATHA700`
5. Paste the customer's Device ID.
6. Choose **Yearly ₹700 • 365 days** or **Free Lifetime**.
7. Tap **GENERATE**, then **COPY CODE**, and send that code to the customer.
8. Customer pastes it under **Plan & Licence** and taps **ACTIVATE**.
